Introduction

The portfolio tells I am a professional Software Developer who has honed my software developing skills over the years and the services have broadened over time, with the craftmanship and professionalism earned me enough.

inspiration

The 3 relevant professional portfolio that has inspired me is the LOKKEE STUDIOS, the Lynn Fisher, and the Augustín Burgos portfolios. What I have learned is how much of an effect using display:grid can have to your portfolio from Lynn Fisher, the interactivity and "circle following the mouse" idea from Augustín Burgos, and the layout ideas of how my portfolio shoudl look from LOKKEE STUDIOS.

Accessibility

Basically I have added a light/dark theme toggle button to change the theme of the website.

Usability

I have added a responsive design on the website to fit the screen of any device. I have implemented a responsive navigation bar to make it easier for user to find what they need. I optimized images, minimized code, and utilized loading techniques to make sure the site loads quickly.

Learning

I had to learn and experiment with display:grid to find out how to make content easier to control and display. I had to learn how to make a hamburger, which when toggled, displays a navbar, and how to make a navigation bar responsive. I also had to learn how to make a circle follow the mouse, expand when hovering over elements, and blend the colors differently.

Evaluation 1

The aspects I thnk are successful were the interactivity and the responsive design of the website.

Evaluation 2

The aspect I think could be improved are the responsive grid design in the projects page.
